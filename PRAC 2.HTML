RECIPE OF APPLE PIE


THIS APPLE PIE RECIPE HAS BEEN IN MY FAMILY FOR GENERATIONS AND NEVER FAILS TO DELIGHT


INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 CUP SUGAR
    • 2 TSP GROUND CINNAMON
  • 6 MEDIUM-SIZED APPLES, PEELED AND SLICED

INSTRUCTIONS


  • PREHEAT OVEN TO 375 F
  • IN A BOWL, MIX THE SUGAR AND CINNAMON
  • TOSS THE APPLES IN THE SUGAR CINNAMON MIXTURE
