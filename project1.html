The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Oppenheimer

The film explores the life of J. Robert Oppenheimer, the physicist who led to the Manhattan Project during World War II and helped create the atomic bomb. The director Christopher Nolan dives deep into the life of Robert J. Oppenheimer and he film delves into his scientific genius, moral dilemmas, and the devastating consequences of his work.
